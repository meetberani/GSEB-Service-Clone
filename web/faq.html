FAQ-this website

➤ What is gujarat board ?
The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB) is the primary education board for the state of Gujarat, India.
➤ How can I join gujarat
register for the SSC (Class 10) or HSC (Class 12) exams through the official website, gseb.org
